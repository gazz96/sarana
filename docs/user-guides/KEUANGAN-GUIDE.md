# Keuangan (Finance) User Guide - SARANAS System

## Overview
As **Keuangan** (Finance), you are responsible for approving budgets and costs for maintenance and repair requests. The SARANAS system helps you review problem costs, approve expenditure, and track maintenance spending.

## üí∞ Budget Approvals

### View Pending Approvals
1. Navigate to **Approvals** ‚Üí **Pending**
2. See problems with status "MENUNGGU PERSETUJUAN KEUANGAN" (7)
3. Each problem shows:
   - **Problem Code**: PRB-XXX
   - **Issue**: Brief description
   - **Total Cost**: Sum of all item costs
   - **Items**: Number of items affected
   - **Submitted By**: Who requested
   - **Days Waiting**: How long awaiting approval

### Review Problem Details
1. Click **"View Details"** on pending problem
2. Review complete information:
   - **Issue Description**: Full problem details
   - **Affected Items**: List with individual costs
   - **Technician Notes**: Repair recommendations
   - **Photos**: Evidence of damage (if available)
   - **Cost Breakdown**: Line-by-line expenses

### Cost Analysis
For each problem, review:
- **Estimated Costs**: Proposed repair costs
- **Item Prices**: Cost per affected item
- **Quantity**: Number of items needing repair
- **Labor Costs**: If applicable
- **Parts/Materials**: Replacement parts needed

### Approve Budget
1. After reviewing, click **"Approve Budget"**
2. Verify the total amount
3. Add approval notes (optional):
   - Budget category to use
   - Payment method
   - Any special instructions
4. Click **"Confirm Approval"**
5. Problem moves to next workflow stage

### Reject Budget
1. If costs are unreasonable:
2. Click **"Reject Budget"**
3. Select rejection reason:
   - **Cost Too High**: Exceeds budget limits
   - **Need More Info**: Insufficient justification
   - **Wrong Category**: Not a maintenance expense
   - **Duplicate Request**: Already approved previously
4. Provide detailed explanation
5. Suggest revision if appropriate
6. Click **"Submit Rejection"**

### Request Revision
1. If you need more information:
2. Click **"Request Revision"**
3. Specify what's needed:
   - Detailed cost breakdown
   - Multiple price quotes
   - Justification for expensive parts
   - Alternative repair options
4. Set response deadline
5. Click **"Request"**

2. See spending categories:

2. View:

   - Budget vs actual spending

### Monthly Reports
1. Navigate to **Reports** ‚Üí **Monthly Spending**
2. Select month and year
3. See detailed breakdown:
   - Total approved amount
   - Problems by category
   - Top spending categories
   - Comparison with previous months

1. Navigate to **Reports** ‚Üí **Export**

3. Choose date range

## üí≥ Payment Processing

### Process Approved Payments
1. After approving budget, process payment:
2. Go to **Payments** ‚Üí **Pending**
3. See approved problems awaiting payment
4. For each problem:
   - **Problem Code**: PRB-XXX
   - **Approved Amount**: Budget you approved
   - **Vendor/Payee**: Who to pay
   - **Payment Method**: How to pay
   - **Due Date**: When payment is needed

### Mark as Paid
1. After making payment:
2. Click **"Mark as Paid"**
3. Enter payment details:
   - **Payment Date**: When paid
   - **Payment Method**: Bank transfer/cash/cheque
   - **Reference Number**: Transaction reference
   - **Notes**: Any additional information
4. Click **"Confirm Payment"**

### Payment History
1. Navigate to **Payments** ‚Üí **History**
2. See all processed payments
3. Filter by date, amount, or vendor

## üîí Financial Controls

### Approval Limits
- **Single Transaction**: Up to Rp 5.000.000
- **Daily Total**: Up to Rp 15.000.000
- **Monthly Total**: Up to allocated budget

### Budget Alerts
You'll be alerted when:
- **90% Budget Used**: Warning to monitor spending
- **100% Budget Reached**: Stop approvals until next month
- **Unusual Costs**: Requests significantly above average

### Audit Trail
All your approvals are logged:
- **Date/Time**: When approved
- **Amount**: How much approved
- **Problem**: What was approved
- **Reason**: Your approval notes

## üõ°Ô∏è Compliance & Procedures

### Approval Checklist
Before approving, verify:
- [ ] Costs are reasonable and justified
- [ ] Problem description is clear
- [ ] Photos support the damage claim
- [ ] Technician recommends the repair
- [ ] Budget is available
- [ ] Correct category is selected

### Rejection Guidelines
Reject when:
- **Excessive Costs**: Request seems overpriced
- **Insufficient Detail**: Can't determine necessity
- **Wrong Category**: Should be different budget
- **Duplicate**: Same problem already approved
- **Unnecessary**: Repair not actually needed

### Documentation
Keep records of:
- Approval decisions and reasons
- Communications with requesters
- Budget adjustments
- Payment confirmations
