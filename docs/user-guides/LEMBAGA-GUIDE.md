## üí∞ Strategic Budgeting

### Approve Major Expenditures
1. Navigate to **Approvals** ‚Üí **Major Projects**
2. See problems requiring management approval:
   - **High-Value Repairs**: Exceeding normal thresholds
   - **Equipment Replacement**: Major purchases
   - **Infrastructure Projects**: Large-scale improvements
   - **Emergency Expenses**: Urgent facility needs

### Review Budget Requests
For each major request:
1. **Problem Details**: What needs to be done
2. **Cost Analysis**: Detailed cost breakdown
3. **Impact Assessment**: Benefits of the expenditure
4. **Alternatives**: Other options considered
5. **Urgency Level**: Timeline for decision

### Make Approval Decisions
1. Review all information
2. Consider strategic impact:
   - **Alignment with Goals**: Does it support educational objectives?
   - **Budget Impact**: Can we afford it?
   - **Priority Level**: How important compared to other needs?
   - **Timing**: Is this the right time?
3. **Approve**: Authorize the expenditure
4. **Reject**: Decline with explanation
5. **Request More Info**: Need additional details

### Long-Term Budget Planning
1. Navigate to **Budget** ‚Üí **Strategic Planning**
2. Plan for future:
   - **Next Quarter**: Upcoming major expenses
   - **Annual Budget**: Yearly maintenance allocation
   - **Capital Projects**: Large infrastructure investments
   - **Reserve Funds**: Emergency budget planning

## üéØ Decision Making

### Major Decisions
As Lembaga, you decide on:
- **Capital Expenditures**: Large equipment purchases
- **Infrastructure Projects**: Building improvements
- **Policy Changes**: Maintenance policy updates
- **Budget Allocations**: How to distribute funds
- **Staffing Levels**: Team size and structure

### Decision Framework
When making decisions:
1. **Analyze Data**: Review available information
2. **Consider Impact**: Short and long-term effects
3. **Consult Team**: Get input from relevant staff
4. **Assess Risk**: Understand potential downsides
5. **Make Decision**: Choose best course of action
6. **Monitor Results**: Track outcome of decision

### Approval Authority
- **Up to Rp 10.000.000**: Your direct approval
- **Rp 10.000.000 - Rp 50.000.000**: Requires board consultation
- **Over Rp 50.000.000**: Full board approval required
